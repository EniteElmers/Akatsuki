Akatsuki Organization

Akatsuki (yap. 暁, literally: "Dawn") — a group of Shinobi that existed outside the usual system of hidden villages. For several decades, Akatsuki has taken various forms and been led by various people. Although each of its members was considered either saboteurs or criminals, they all sought to make the world a better place to live according to their own vision.
